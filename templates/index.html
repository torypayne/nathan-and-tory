The Fairmont is at the top of a steep hill, and while we often walk there, you may not want to. We recommend using Uber, Lyft, or public transportation to get there. If you plan on taking the Powell Street cable car to the wedding, we advise against boarding at Powell and Market--it's a tourist hotspot and sometimes has an hour plus wait to board.

Lodging

We've held a limited number of rooms at the Fairmont for $199/night (single queen bed) or $259/night (two queen beds) on March 3rd and 4th. You need to book through the special event site to recieve the discounted rate.

Alternatively, hotels in the Fisherman's Wharf area may be a cheaper option. Airbnb will probably have the cheapest options (as well as some nicer, more expensive ones). If you've never used Airbnb, you can use Tory's referral link to save $35 on your first stay.
